The pipeline processes post-mission UAV footage through 4 stages and 11 components. At sub-50 px person scales, pixel-based methods fail (MViTv2-S: 26.5%) while trajectory-centric streams (TMS-16: 60.3%) operate resolution-independently on centroid geometry alone. The AAI-v2 meta-classifier adaptively weights streams based on person size, routing small persons to trajectory analysis and large persons to pixel analysis.
